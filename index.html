About Us

Tiger Team Analytics LLC is a Charleston, South Carolina-based consulting firm specializing in data engineering and data analytics. We serve both government and private sector organizations, helping them navigate the complexities of modern enterprise data landscapes and unlock the transformative potential of artificial intelligence.

Our name comes from the military tradition of "tiger teams" - highly specialized groups assembled to identify vulnerabilities and solve critical problems. But our mission goes beyond simply finding weaknesses. Like the biblical principle in Proverbs 27:17, "Iron sharpens iron; so a man sharpens the countenance of his friend," we work alongside our clients to strengthen their organizations through transparency, expertise, and ethical stewardship.

We operate with precision and efficiency - coming in, solving complex data challenges, and delivering results quickly. Our work represents the foundation upon which AI agents and intelligent systems are built. By establishing robust data infrastructure, comprehensive governance frameworks, and clear visibility into data flows, we catapult organizations' AI capabilities forward. You cannot build effective AI without understanding where your data lives, how it moves, and whether you can trust it - that's where we come in.
